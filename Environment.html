The Environment

Weaver ants are so incredibly beneficial to the environment.

These tiny but amazing creatures help humans, animals and plants alike. Many studies from reputable institutions like Oxford and Harvard have found that weaver ants can be beneficial in so many more ways then just pets. They are helpful to farmers, and any home owner who own fruit trees who struggle with pests. Ants are known for being great pest removers, if you dont mind them yourself. A major study showed that weaver ants being used in orchards and farms prevents pests from eating produce, and helps contribute to sales.
